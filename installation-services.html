INSTALLATION SERVICES

At ShadeCo, our expert installation services ensure seamless setup of tensile fabric structures, canopies, and other shade solutions. From initial planning to final execution, our skilled installation team collaborates with engineers to deliver hassle-free, high-quality results tailored to your project needs. Every project is unique, and the installation of tensile membrane structures varies based on shape, size, and design. The engineering team at ShadeCo is well equipped with the techniques and makes use of machinery to ensure proper installation of the tensile structures.

Our in-house installation team is trained from time to time and has a strong understanding of the concepts and the tools used during the installation process. Some equipment that can be used to assist in the installation of tensile structures includes cranes, engineering forklifts, booms, scaffolding and scissor lifts. Our offsite construction capabilities and the unique properties of tensile membranes minimize on-site build time and disruption while maintaining exceptional quality.

CERTIFIED QUALITY AND SAFETY STANDARDS

At ShadeCo, our ISO 9001:2015 certification for the “Design, Manufacture, and Installation of Fabric Structures” reflects our commitment to excellence. This globally recognized standard ensures our tensile fabric structure processes meet rigorous quality management standards, delivering consistent, reliable, and high-quality results for every project.

  • Strict Adherence to Industry-Leading Standards for Structural Design and Installation.
  • Comprehensive Health and Safety Plans, Risk Assessments, and Method Statements.
  • Safe and Precise Equipment Use for Complex Installations.
  • Structural Steel and Aluminum Components Meet IS Code Standards for Safety and Durability.
