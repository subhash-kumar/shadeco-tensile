ACP CLADDING SOLUTIONS BY SHADECO

ACP cladding, also known as Aluminium Composite Panel cladding is a leading architectural material that’s used for building front elevation (facades), interiors, signage, modular kitchens, and more. It features two aluminium sheets bonded to a core, typically polyethylene (PE) or fire-retardant (FR) mineral-filled material. ACP cladding offers stunning aesthetics with various colours, textures, and finishes. It’s the perfect choice for residential, commercial, and industrial spaces.

At ShadeCo, we are India’s trusted experts in delivering high-quality Aluminium Composite Panel solutions. With years of experience, we specialize in ACP sheet work, cladding, and panel installations for homes, offices, and industrial spaces across India. Our commitment to excellence makes us the top choice for innovative and reliable ACP cladding services.
