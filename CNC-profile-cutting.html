CNC PROFILE CUTTING AND FOLDING

At ShadeCo, we deliver top-tier CNC profile cutting and folding services to meet the diverse needs of our clients, using cutting-edge technology and equipment. Our CNC profile cutting and folding services are ideal for a wide range of industries, including construction, manufacturing, automotive and among others.

We have the flexibility to work on projects of all sizes and complexities, from small components to large roofing structures. Our certified welders specialise in welding processes such as MIG, Pulse MIG, Flux Core, Ark, TIG, gas and plasma cutting, Arc air gouging, and more.
