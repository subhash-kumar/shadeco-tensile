THE SECOND STAGE

ARCHITECTURAL DESIGN AND ENGINEERING

contact now

ShadeCo is your trusted partner for innovative design and engineering solutions, specializing in lightweight, elegant tensile structures. Powered by cutting-edge software, we transform visionary ideas into reality, delivering exceptional results across every phase of project development. Our dedicated team of experienced engineers and designers brings creativity and expertise to ensure your project’s success.

Our Tensile Fabric Membrane Design Process:

Form-Finding: Through an iterative process, our designers craft the optimal membrane shape, balancing aesthetics and functionality to achieve the perfect form.

Static and Dynamic Equilibrium: We meticulously analyse load combinations to ensure structural integrity, evaluating deflections to create safe, reliable designs.

Patterning: Our advanced process converts pre-stressed 3D membranes into precise 2D patterns, enabling seamless structural implementation.

Tensile structure or tensile membrane structure is a structure that is characterized by a tensioning of the fabric or pliable material system typically with wire or cable to provide the critical structural support to the structure.

The frame and cables are typically constructed with steel. The cables will either be stainless steel or GI coated with a clear PVC finish to protect against corrosion. The steel frame will be finished with a smooth layer of powder coat or Painted over a base of primer. If the structure is located near a harsh marine environment, then an epoxy wet paint finish is recommended.
The roof covers are usually made of high tensioned architectural fabrics such as PVC, PTFE & ETFE. PVC Fabric is a high strength architectural fabric Most commonly used in the construction of tensile structures. PVC materials are manufactured by a variety of reputed companies such as Sattler PRO-TEX GmbH, Serge Ferrari, Sioen, Mehler Texnologies, and SRF Limited.
